Whether 让步状语从句的倒装

whether 引导让步状语从句，如果谓语动词是Be动词，则可以改成be开头的倒装句

  • All substances, whether they ==are== gaseous, liquid or solid, are made up of atoms.正常句型

  • All substances, whether they ==be== gaseous, liquid or solid, are made up of atoms.倒装句

  • All substances, ==be== they gaseous, liquid or solid, are made up of atoms.倒装句​​
